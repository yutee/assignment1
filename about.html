About Me

Who?

Hello world, I am Utibe Okon. Born in 1996 to Nigerian parents, I was also brought up in the nigerian way. I studied Computer Engineering in the University of Uyo, Uyo. I am passionate about computers and curious about how they work. I spend most of my time trying to figure things out with a computer, When I get tired, i resort to playing and following sports.

Why?

Despite spending most of my adult life with computers, I have always struggled to find focus in learning and mastering one of the many fields of computing. When I heard about Alt school, a structured learning program, I thought it will be the key to mastering a field of computing that would best suite me. I decided to enroll and follow through, and so far it has been everything I hoped for.

Plans?

I have always wanted to contribute my qouta to the fast emerging field of computing and technology, and I hope that by the end of my program with Alt School, I will be able to garner all the necesssary skills to do just that. I also plan to reinvest time, money and effort in helping people around me grow.
